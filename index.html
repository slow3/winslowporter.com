About

Based in New York, Winslow Porter produces works that blend narrative design, technical experimentation, and social impact. His projects emphasize embodiment, empathy, and environmental awareness through VR, AR, and mixed reality.

  • Immersive production leadership from concept to release.
  • Cross-disciplinary collaboration across artists, technologists, and institutions.
  • Story-first prototyping with real-time media pipelines.
