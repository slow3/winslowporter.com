Bio

Winslow Porter produces immersive media that blends narrative design, technical experimentation, and social impact. His work spans VR, AR, mixed reality, and film installations, with recurring themes around empathy, environmental awareness, and embodied audience participation.

Public profiles and project credits list him as producer/co-creator of Giant and Tree, co-founder/executive producer at New Reality Company, and collaborator on CLOUDS and Forager.
